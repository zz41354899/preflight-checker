# Work Handoff

Use for work deliverables, task handoff, status updates, specs, QA notes, files, collaboration messages, and delivery packages.

## Inspect For

- objective: what is being delivered and why
- owner: who owns next action, review, approval, or follow-up
- status: done, pending, blocked, needs decision, needs review
- scope: included items, excluded items, assumptions, constraints
- artifacts: file names, links, versions, locations, permissions
- quality checks: acceptance criteria, test result, known issue, risk
- timeline: deadline, date, timezone, next checkpoint
- handoff gap: what the receiver may not know without context

## Common Risks

- no clear next action
- missing file or access permission
- ambiguous "done"
- unmentioned blocker or dependency
- old version sent as final
- no owner for review or approval
- hidden assumption that affects delivery

## Output Notes

Compare the current handoff against a clear receiver-ready version. Prioritize P0 for blockers that prevent action, P1 for clarity or delivery risk, and P2 for polish.
