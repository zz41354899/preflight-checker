# Message Email

Use for Email, LINE, DM, Slack, outreach, replies, reminders, follow-ups, and external messages.

## Inspect For

- recipient: who receives it and what relationship they have with the sender
- purpose: ask, update, confirm, decline, remind, follow up, hand off
- context: what the receiver needs to know before acting
- action: what exactly the receiver should do next
- deadline: date, time, timezone, response expectation
- tone: polite, direct, warm, firm, internal, external
- attachments/links: included, named, accessible
- risk: ambiguity, overlong message, wrong channel, missing courtesy, sensitive info

## Common Risks

- request is buried after too much context
- deadline is vague
- no explicit next action
- tone is too stiff, too casual, or too apologetic
- attachment or link is referenced but missing
- external message assumes internal knowledge
- sensitive details are sent to the wrong audience

## Output Notes

Use comparison to revise the message from receiver's point of view. Keep suggested wording ready to send when possible.
